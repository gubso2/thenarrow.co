What does The Narrow include?

Every feature exists to help you resist temptation and replace it with something stronger.

Remove & Replace Temptation

Porn Blocker

Block access to pornography across your devices. A first line of defense that removes temptation before it reaches you.

Routines

Build healthy habits that replace temptation with intentional actions — scripture, prayer, exercise, or connection with a brother.

Resist Temptation

Community

Connect with brothers walking the same path. Share your journey, encourage one another, and fight together.

Panic Button

When temptation hits, one tap brings immediate help — scripture, a redirect, or a prompt to reach your accountability brother.

Check in daily with your progress. Consistent accountability builds the discipline that keeps you on the narrow path.

Invite a trusted brother to walk alongside you. Share your streak, send an SOS when you're struggling, and receive real accountability.

Daily Scripture

Truth delivered when you need it most. Daily verses and wisdom to anchor your heart and mind.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Evidence-based exercises to identify triggers, challenge harmful thought patterns, and build lasting change.
