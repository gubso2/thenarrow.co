Pornography is killing the church.

It's not a fringe issue. Studies suggest that 50–70% of Christian men report struggling with pornography, and research from Barna shows many practicing Christians engage with it regularly — often in silence.

The impact isn't just personal. It weakens marriages, sidelines leaders, and erodes spiritual confidence within congregations.

Churches want to respond — but often lack practical structure.

We exist to help churches address this wisely, equipping men with daily tools and accountability so the Church grows stronger from the inside out.
