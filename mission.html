OUR MISSION

To call men off the wide road of pornography and onto the Narrow Path — into strength, freedom, and the fullness of God's design.

One in two Christian men quietly battle pornography — losing focus, intimacy, and drive. The wide road promises temporary ease, but it leads somewhere empty.

We believe men can choose a different path. So we build tools grounded in proven psychology and timeless truth — streak tracking, habit replacement, daily reflection, and real accountability. The Narrow exists for one purpose: to help men quit porn and reclaim the fullness of life.

This is the narrow path.

Our Story

Cam has served as a Christian youth leader for years. He watched young men who loved God quietly struggle with pornography — trapped in shame, secrecy, and confusion. The silence around it was costing them their confidence, relationships, and spiritual fire.

Yujin's story is harder. Regrettably, 10 years ago, he made criminal choices that led to prison for consuming illegal content — something Yujin regrets daily and feels deep remorse for.

Through accountability, repentance, and radical change, his life was rebuilt. Today, he is committed to stopping others from engaging in this destructive industry.

The Narrow exists because God's grace is stronger than shame — and you were never meant to live chained to secrecy, guilt, and compulsive desire.

Built on Grace

Freedom doesn't start with shame — it starts with grace. That's why every message inside The Narrow is grounded in truth, but delivered with grace.

Based on Science

Lasting change requires more than motivation. The Narrow applies behavioural science, habit formation, and neuroscience-backed principles to help you rewire patterns and build discipline that endures.

Backed by Action

100% of profits are committed to organisations actively fighting child sexual exploitation — reducing demand, dismantling networks, and protecting the vulnerable.
